# SHA Fingerprints Resources

## Knowledge

- [Firebase Docs: Authenticating Your Client — SHA Certificate Fingerprints](https://firebase.google.com/docs/android/setup#add-config-file)
  Official source for why Firebase needs SHA and how to add fingerprints. Use for: setup steps and the "why" in Android context.

- [Android Docs: Sign Your App](https://developer.android.com/studio/publish/app-signing)
  Explains debug vs release keystores, how Android signing works. Use for: understanding what key is being fingerprinted.

- [NIST FIPS 180-4 — Secure Hash Standard](https://nvlpubs.nist.gov/nistpubs/FIPS/NIST.FIPS.180-4.pdf)
  The official specification for SHA-1, SHA-256, and SHA-512. Use for: authoritative reference (technical/heavy).

- [Computerphile: SHA — Secure Hashing Algorithm (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DMtFhACPnTY)
  Excellent visual explainer of how SHA-256 works conceptually. Recommended first watch. Use for: intuition building.

- [How HTTPS Works — SHA and Certificate Fingerprints](https://howhttps.works/)
  Comic-style walkthrough of TLS. Use for: seeing SHA fingerprints in the TLS/SSL context.

## Wisdom (Communities)

- [Stack Overflow: android+firebase+sha](https://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/firebase+sha-1)
  High-volume Q&A on the exact Firebase SHA setup issues. Use for: debugging specific errors.
